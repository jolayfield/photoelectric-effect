# Week 13 — Polyatomic Molecules & Electronic Spectroscopy

**Reading:** Engel, Chapters 13 & 14

## Overview

We extend MO theory to polyatomic molecules using Hückel theory for conjugated π systems, apply group theory for orbital construction, and then explore electronic spectroscopy — UV-Vis absorption, fluorescence, phosphorescence, and the Franck-Condon principle.
